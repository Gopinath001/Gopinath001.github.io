why?.. you may ask

i was experimenting with some stuff relating to sim card tray.. that involved removing and inserting the micro sd card rapidly. i never believed in safely un-mounting the sd card.. BOY did i learn a lesson here.

repeated tampering with the card made the card fail.. #rip

Cause
